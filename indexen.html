a little map


A little map is a very small, independent research business based in Athens, Greece. Founded in 2018 to support the research work of Stathis G. Arapostathis, it has since operated with modest but steady profit, offering a flexible framework for research, writing, and digital dissemination. Key areas of interest include Social Media, Machine Learning, Natural Disasters, Crisis Events, GIS, Volunteered Geographic Information, Tourism Geography, and Geopolitics — often explored through a lens of policy relevance and open knowledge. Advanced digital infrastructure underpins a little map’s research activities, with ongoing upgrades that ensure flexibility, scalability, and continuity even under challenging conditions. While maintaining autonomy, A little map remains open to selective collaboration, especially where shared goals can enhance applied, interdisciplinary work.

  • Natural Disasters and Crisis Events.

  • Research activities regarding Geography and Environment.

  • Educational activities through internet.

  • Research and Technology.
