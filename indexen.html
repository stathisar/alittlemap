Welcome to a little map

Based in Athens, Greece, a little map is a very small research and education brand that is functional since August 2018. A little map conducts research, and generates educational content, having also collaborations with organisations and individuals in Greece and abroad.

What a little map offers:

  • Research & Analysis: Interdisciplinary research across social media, disaster management, crisis management, natural disasters, geography, tourism, environmental studies, geopolitics etc.
  • GIS, Mapping & Data Solutions: Design, implementation, and analysis of location-based projects for international clients and cross-border collaborations.
  • Educational Support: Online materials, tools, and guidance for learners, professionals, and institutions worldwide.
  • Collaborative Solutions: Tailored partnerships to address specific needs with agility and independence.

Background

The very first revenue in the history of the business came from the Russian Embassy in Greece in 2018. This was for a mapping application created to honor the Russian victims of the Second World War, in the Greek area. During that period there wasn't any specific brand. The app remained online for some time, during the start of the recent war conflicts, it was eventually retired from the embassy’s webpage.

That small yet significant project was the beginning of the a little map’s journey. Since then, operations have expanded through collaborations with various public sector bodies of Greece, primarily via registrations as an individual professional. At the same time, the business has grown its international presence by engaging diverse clients worldwide through platforms like Upwork.

In 2020, the brand “a little map” was officially registered with the Athens Chamber of Commerce.
