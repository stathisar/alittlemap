a little map


The a little map is a very small, independent research business based in Athens, Greece. Founded in August 2018 to support the research work of Dr. Stathis G. Arapostathis — a researcher and recently, a secondary education teacher in Greece. In Q4 2018 received its first revenue, from the Russian Embassy of Greece. Since Q1 2019 a little map has been operating with annual modest profit, steadily, offering a flexible framework for research, writing, and digital dissemination. Key areas of interest include Social Media, Machine Learning, Natural Disasters, Crisis Events, GIS, Volunteered Geographic Information, Tourism Geography, and Geopolitics — often explored through a lens of policy relevance and open knowledge. Advanced digital infrastructure underpins a little map’s research activities, with ongoing upgrades that ensure flexibility, scalability, and continuity even under challenging conditions. While maintaining autonomy, a little map remains open to selective collaboration, especially where shared goals can enhance applied, interdisciplinary work. International collaborations can be ensured through upwork, either through the profile of Dr. Stathis G. Arapostathis or through the newly established one of the a little map agency.


    Indicative research interests:

  • Scientific Research in Social Media.

  • Natural Disasters and Crisis Events.

  • Research activities regarding Geography and Environment.

  • Educational activities through internet.

  • Research and Technology.

  • Tourism Geography.
